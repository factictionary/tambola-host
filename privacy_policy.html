Privacy Policy

Tambola Housie Host  ·  Last updated: June 17, 2026
In short: Tambola Housie Host does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device, works fully offline, and does not require an account, sign-up, or internet connection.

1. Who we are

Tambola Housie Host ("the App", "we", "us") is developed and published by an independent developer. For any questions about this policy or the App, you can reach us at factictionary@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically, the App:

  • Does not require you to create an account or sign in
  • Does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information
  • Does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, or files
  • Does not use analytics, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs of any kind
  • Does not connect to the internet during normal use — all number calling, ticket generation, and prize detection happen entirely on your device

3. Data storage

Any game settings (such as your preferred calling interval or voice on/off preference) and any tickets you generate are stored locally on your device only, using standard on-device app storage. This information is never transmitted anywhere and is automatically removed if you uninstall the App.

4. Advertising

If you are using a version of this App that displays advertisements, those ads are served via Google AdMob, which may collect a limited set of technical identifiers (such as your device's advertising ID) to show relevant ads and measure ad performance, in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. You can review Google's practices at policies.google.com/privacy and you can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device's settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads, or equivalent, depending on your device).

If you downloaded a build of this App that does not include advertisements, this section does not apply, and no advertising identifiers are collected.

5. Children's privacy

Tambola Housie Host does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children. Because the App requires no account and no personal information of any kind, it does not knowingly process personal data from children under 13 (or the relevant minimum age in your jurisdiction).

6. Third-party services

Other than the optional advertising SDK described in Section 4, this App does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or backend servers. There is no database, no cloud storage, and no server component of any kind.

7. Your rights

Because we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers (we have no servers), there is no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. Any data generated by the App lives only on your device and is fully within your control — you can clear it at any time by resetting the game within the App or by uninstalling the App entirely.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if we add new features that change how the App works. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please contact us at factictionary@gmail.com.
